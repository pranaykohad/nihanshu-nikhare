About Nihanshu Nikhare

3+ Years of
Practice

Principal Architect

Nihanshu
Nikhare

Nihanshu Nikhare is an architect and designer whose work explores the delicate relationship between built form and human experience. With over 3 years of practice across residential, commercial, and cultural projects, his studio has earned a reputation for architecture that is both intellectually rigorous and deeply humane.

Trained at the Priyadarshini Institute of Architecture and Design Studies, Nagpur, and later at the Architectural Association in Nagpur, Nihanshu brings a cross-cultural sensibility to every project — honouring local materiality while embracing progressive global design thinking.

Design Philosophy

"Good architecture is never about the architect. It is about the light at 6 AM, the breeze through a window in July, the feeling of arriving home. We design for that invisible layer — the lived experience between walls."
